How it started

We got tired of settling for average

Section started in 2024 when our founders couldn't find a store that truly understood quality. Every other option was either overpriced luxury or cheap garbage — nothing in between.

So we built it ourselves. A curated space where every item earns its place. No filler, no fluff — just products we'd actually buy ourselves.
